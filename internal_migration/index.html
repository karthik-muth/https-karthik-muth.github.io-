But this wasn't the case forty years go. In 1981, half of the people lived in 42 municipalities. Since then ten municipalities have disappeared from the list as people moved to the bigger cities. As an exception, Kungsbacka –which wasn't in the list in 1981– is now part of the top 33 most concentrated municipalities of Sweden.

Four more municipalities –Kalmar, Luleå, Skellefteå och Sollentuna–will leave this list in the next forty years, according to Rasmus Andersson, a statistician at Statistics Sweden.
